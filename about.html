Our Story

Pioneering Research Peptide Supply

31-32 Peptides was founded with a clear mission: to provide the scientific research community with access to the highest quality peptides available. We recognised a gap in the market for a supplier that combines rigorous quality standards with knowledgeable customer support and reliable service.

Every product in our catalogue has been carefully selected and sourced to meet the demanding requirements of modern research laboratories. From individual compounds to precision-formulated blends, we ensure that each peptide meets our exacting purity and quality benchmarks.
